# Session 13 - From Forms to Agents with Gemini & Angular

- **Speakers**: [Joseph Masaka](https://www.linkedin.com/in/josephmasaka/)

## About the session
Google I/O 2026 marked a shift from AI that assists to AI that acts. In this hands-on session, I will demonstrate how developers can integrate Gemini 3.5 into Angular applications to build intelligent business workflows that go beyond traditional chat experiences.

Using a real-world HR and asset management application, attendees will see how AI can automate employee onboarding, process leave requests, analyze documents, generate reports, and support asset allocation decisions. The session includes a live demo showing how Gemini 3.5 can work alongside existing Laravel APIs and enterprise data to create practical AI agents for organizations across Kenya and Africa.

Participants will learn how to integrate Gemini into Angular applications, design AI-powered workflows, connect AI to existing APIs and databases, and apply best practices for building enterprise-ready AI solutions.

By the end of the session, attendees will have a practical blueprint for adding AI agent capabilities to their own applications.
