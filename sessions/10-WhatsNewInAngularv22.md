# Session 10 - What's New in Angular v22

- **Speakers**: [Wayne Gakuo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/waynegakuo/)

## About the session
In this session, we will explore the next era of web development as Angular positions itself as a rock-solid foundation for both human developers and autonomous AI. We will dive deep into the major framework updates dropping in Angular v22, focusing on crucial template enhancements like the new `<boundary>` tag for isolated error handling, alongside the official graduation of Signal Forms and Angular Aria to stable status. Finally, we will look at the cutting edge of "Vibe Coding" and agentic development, showcasing how the new Model Context Protocol (MCP) and optimized Agent Skills allow AI to seamlessly build, test, and visually verify production-ready Angular applications.
